#+title: Riemannian Metric

--------------

aliases: - Riemannian metric - Riemannian manifold
---

A *Riemannian metric* on a [[id:58660F1E-EB64-4506-81E9-63FF79BD2E76][manifold]] \(M\) is a smooth symmetric covariant \(2\)-[[id:67465580-C555-4F2E-842B-1E8814F831C4][tensor field]] that is positive definite at each point. A *Riemannian manifold* is a pair \((M, g)\), where \(M\) is a smooth manifold and \(g\) is a Riemannian metric.

In smooth local coordinates \((x^i)\), a Riemannian metric can be written
\[g = g_{ij}\,dx^i\otimes dx^j.\]
