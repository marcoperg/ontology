#+title: Topological Vector Spaces

--------------

aliases: - topological vector space
---

A topological vector space (VTS) is a [[id:C31DD475-75D9-4BB9-9DD5-536453386F9D][vector space]] with an endowed [[id:C0ADBA68-2416-4041-A4E8-E3F3778D9938][topology]] such that the operations of scalar multiplication and vector addition are continuous w.r.t that topology.
