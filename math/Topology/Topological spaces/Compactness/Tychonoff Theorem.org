#+title: Tychonoff Theorem

Given [[id:8E24A191-E911-4243-8DDA-3404B256A715][compact]] spaces \(\{X_\alpha\}_{\alpha \in J}\) its [[id:22783D02-0670-4A34-B1FA-EDD8C5139B77][product]] is compact.

This theorem is equivalent to the [[id:E4068E5E-6D76-4C89-B4EC-22E84E23478F][Zorn's Lemma]].
