#+title: Open mapping principle
#+SETUPFILE: ../../../setup.org

Let $T: E\to F$ be a surjective continuous linear map between [[id:29768ACE-BD6A-47E1-9E63-EAFAEDB474AA][Banach spaces]]. Then $T$ is an open mapping, i.e. $T(U)$ open for all open $U\subset E$.
