* Young's convolution inequality

Take $f$ in $L^p(\mathbb{R}^d)$ and $g$ in $L^{p'}(\mathbb{R}^d)$ and
$$\frac{1}{p} + \frac{1}{p'} = \frac{1}{r} + 1$$

with $1\le p, q,r \le \infty$. Then

$$\Vert f * g \Vert_r \le \Vert f\Vert_g \Vert g \Vert_q.$$
