#+title: Bessel's inequality
#+SETUPFILE: ../../setup.org

Let $\{ x_\theta : \theta \in I\}$ be an orthonormal famility in a [[id:E5B1D38D-297B-4867-91DA-75156F747A92][Hilbert space]] $X$ (or pre-hilbert). Then, for all $x\in X$, let $\alpha_\theta = \langle x, x_\theta\rangle$:

\begin{itemize}
\item $\{ \theta : \alpha_\theta \ne 0\}$ is at most contable.
\item $\sum_{\theta\in I$ \vert \alpha_\theta \vert^2 \le \Vert x\Vert^2$
\end{itemize}
