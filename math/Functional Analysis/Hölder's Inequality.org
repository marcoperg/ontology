* Statement of the theorem

\begin{theorem}
Let $f,g$ be functions in $L^p(\mathbb{K})$, where $\mathbb{K} = \mathbb{R}\text{ or } \mathbb{C}$, for a given integer $p \in \mathbb{R} \backslash \{0\}$. Moreover, we denote by $p'$ the number such that $\frac{1}{p} + \frac{1}{p'} = 1$. Then we have

$$
\Vert fg\Vert_1 \le \Vert f\Vert_p \Vert g \Vert_{p'}
$$
\end{theorem}

The space $L^p(\mathbb{K})$ is an [[id:6685FCEC-5551-44BF-9E23-50CD4F65DEFC][Lp space]].
