#+title: Gauge functions
#+SETUPFILE: ../../setup.org

Given a normed vector space $(X, \Vert, \cdot, \Vert)$.
Let $K$ be convex subset of $X$. Take $x$ in the a [[id:6006B53B-A2EA-458F-A7F8-63ABCDFCE9C0][algebraic interior]] of K. Define
\begin{align*}
p_{K, x}: X &\longrightarrow \mathbb{R}^+ \\
y &\longmapsto \inf\left\{ a>0 \,:\, x + \frac{1}{a} y \in K\right\}
\end{align*}

Note that $p_{K, x}(y) < \infty$. Such a function is called a Gauge function.
