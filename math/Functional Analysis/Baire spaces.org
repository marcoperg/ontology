#+title: Baire spaces
#+SETUPFILE: ../../setup.org

* Definition

A [[id:C0ADBA68-2416-4041-A4E8-E3F3778D9938][topological space]] is said to be a *Baire space* if for all sequence $(E_n)_{n\in \mathbb{N}}$ of dense open subsets we have

$$\cup_{n\in \mathbb{N}} E_n$$

is also dense.

* Baire category theorem

** All complete pseudo-metric spaces are Baire spaces.
** All [[id:8E24A191-E911-4243-8DDA-3404B256A715][locally-compact]] [[id:C5EBDB18-8574-431C-A794-07E2BAADF831][regular]] space are Baire spaces.
