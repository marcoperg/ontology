#+title: Key relay

The key relay operation consist of the transmission from node \(A_1\) to node \(A_n\) of a key through nodes \(A_2, ... A_{n-1}\) that acts as [[id:04E3709B-5EA6-4DB0-9AF1-9C56C8F713DB][trusted nodes]].
